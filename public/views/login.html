Do you like Books?

If not, surely you know someone who does. Our goal in creating Book Buddies was to find an easier way to trade books with people in your local area. We’ve creating an app that allows you to add books to your own personal library and store them to be traded with other users on our site.

Trade your books with other Bibliophiles

Our app uses the Google Books API, so just about any books you can image from textbooks to comic books can be included.Our app proposes the trade and sends notifications to traders through email. All your information is kept private and you’re allowed to trade on your own terms.The app is fully functional, however, we have big ideas ideas for its future.
